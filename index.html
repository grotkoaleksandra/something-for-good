Every grant goes straight to one person to use however they decide. An unrestricted gift, given with trust. We back two kinds of people: those who keep showing up for others, and those facing a hard moment when a small amount changes what happens next.

How it works

Three rules, and we keep them simple.

We move small amounts of money to the right person fast, and we stay out of the way once we do.

01

We trust the person

The grant has no conditions. People who spend their days helping others have earned the freedom to decide what helps most.

02

We stay local

Our leads come from teachers, nurses, social workers, and neighborhood organizers who already know who is making a difference where they live.

03

We keep vetting light

The people closest to a community usually know who is holding it together. A short, respectful check confirms what they already see.
