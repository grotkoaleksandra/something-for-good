Privacy

Privacy Policy

We collect very little, keep it only as long as we need it, and never sell it. Here is exactly how it works.

Last updated: July 2026

Who we are

Something For Good is a nonprofit that gives small grants directly to people. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and by email, how we use it, and the choices you have. Questions about anything here can be sent to somethingforgoodfund@gmail.com.

What we collect

When you submit a nomination, we collect what you type into the form:

  • Your name and email address, so we can follow up with you.
  • The name and general location of the person you are nominating, how you know them, and what you tell us about them.

If you email us directly, we receive whatever you choose to include. This website does not use analytics trackers or advertising cookies, and does not run accounts or logins.

Information about nominees

Nominations describe a person who may not know they were nominated. We treat that information with particular care. We use it only to evaluate the nomination, we do not publish it, and we contact the nominator before ever contacting a nominee. If we do not move forward with a nomination, we delete the information rather than keeping it on file.

How nominations reach us

The nomination form is delivered by Formspree, a form-processing service, which transmits your submission to our email inbox. We have configured Formspree not to retain submissions after delivery. Formspree's own privacy practices are described at formspree.io. This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log standard technical data such as IP addresses as part of operating the service.

How we use information

  • To review nominations and make grant decisions.
  • To communicate with nominators and, where appropriate, nominees.
  • To respond to messages sent to us.

We do not sell personal information, and we do not share it with third parties except the service providers named above, or where the law requires it.

Stories and publicity

We share grant recipients' stories publicly only with their permission. Anyone who prefers privacy keeps it.

How long we keep information

We keep nomination information only while it is being considered and, for grants that are made, basic records needed for the organization's bookkeeping and legal obligations. Information about nominations we decline is deleted.

Your choices

You can ask us at any time to see, correct, or delete information you have sent us by writing to somethingforgoodfund@gmail.com. If you are a nominee and would like the information submitted about you removed, tell us and we will delete it.

Children

This website is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will post the updated version on this page with a new date.
